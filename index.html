Dino Puzzles — Privacy Policy

Simple, clear, and kid-safe.

👤 Who We Are

Dino Puzzles is a children's language learning app developed and maintained by Paskal Brainstormer. If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at: paskalishteto@gmail.com

📱 Data Stored on Your Device

Dino Puzzles stores the following information locally on your device only, using your device's own storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your phone and is never transmitted to any server operated by us.

  • The child's name and optional family names (mama, papa) — used to personalise the experience
  • Language preference, voice choice, and selected age group
  • Star count and learning progress
  • Ad session counter (used to control how often ads are shown)

✅ We do not operate any servers. We collect no personal data ourselves.

📢 Advertising — Google AdMob

Dino Puzzles displays ads served by Google AdMob. Because this app is designed for children, we have enabled the following child-safety flags in our AdMob configuration:

  • Child-directed treatment is enabled — limits personalised ad targeting
  • Under-age-of-consent treatment is enabled

Google may collect certain device information (such as an advertising ID) to serve ads. You can review Google's privacy practices at policies.google.com/privacy.

Users who purchase the "Remove Ads" option will not be shown any advertisements.

💳 In-App Purchases — Google Play Billing

The app offers a one-time optional purchase called "Remove Ads", processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not receive, store, or process any payment card details. All transaction handling is managed by Google.

You can review Google Play's terms at play.google.com/about/play-terms.

👶 Children's Privacy

Dino Puzzles is designed for children of all ages. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children on any server
  • Names entered in the app are stored only on the child's own device
  • All ad serving uses child-directed treatment as required by applicable law
  • We do not use behavioural tracking or profiling on child users

🔒 Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, correct, or delete your data.

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data in your Android settings, or by uninstalling the app.

For any privacy-related requests, contact us at: YOUR-EMAIL@HERE.COM

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
